Introduction

Bsc Physical Science is a three year Undergraduate programme course in Delhi University.This focuses on three core subjects listed below:

  1. Physics
  2. Maths
  3. Computer science or Chemistry

Here we'll be talking only about Physical Science with Cs..

Requirements

This requires highest education to be 10+2 and the student shoud have studied Physics,Chemistry and Maths.

Curriculum

The Curriculum for three semesters is written below

:
Subjects Semester 1 Semester 2 Semester 3
Physics Mechanics Electricity and Magnetism Optics
Maths Calculus Linear Algebra Combinatorics
CS Programming with c++ Data Structures Python for analysis

Extra Subjects

Three Extra subjects are also included for overall enhancement of student which includes SEC,VAC and GE.There are numerous options available for these:

Options for SEC

  • Web Designing
  • Data Analysis Using Python
  • Cyber Security
  • Photography and Videography
  • and many more...

Options for VAC

  • Yoga and Meditation
  • Communication and Personality Development
  • Vedic Mathematics
  • Digital Wellness
  • and many more...

Options for GE

  • Environmental Science
  • Introduction to Psychology
  • Indian Polity and Governance
  • Basics of Artificial Intelligence
  • and many more...

Using Ge you can get a minor degree in any of three core subjects and you have to choose DSE to take major degree or honours degree in subject you want.
